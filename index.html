About Us

Welcome to Exiton Exim, your trusted partner in the global trade of premium jaggery and agricultural products. Based in India, we specialize in the import and export of high-quality jaggery, along with a variety of other farm produce such as spices, grains, and fresh vegetables. Our focus is on delivering the natural sweetness and richness of jaggery, known for its health benefits, to international markets. At Exiton Exim, our mission is to connect Indian jaggery producers with global buyers, ensuring that the wholesome, traditional sweetness of India's agricultural heritage reaches every corner of the world. We are deeply committed to quality, sustainability, and ethical sourcing, working closely with farmers and suppliers to provide fresh, organic, and high-grade jaggery products. With our expertise in logistics, compliance, and international trade, we ensure seamless transactions and timely delivery, making the import and export of jaggery and agricultural products a smooth and reliable experience. We focus on maintaining long-term partnerships built on trust, reliability, and excellence. Empowering Indian Agriculture, Connecting Global Markets!

Popular Products

Jaggery Tea

Jaggery tea, made by adding unrefined sugar (jaggery) to tea, offers several health benefits, including boosting immunity, aiding digestion, providing sustained energy, and supporting respiratory health.

Jaggery sweet dish

Jaggery-sweetened dishes offer potential health benefits like improved digestion, iron absorption, and energy levels, compared to refined sugar, due to its rich mineral and antioxidant content.

Jaggery cube and powder

Jaggery, whether in cube or powder form, offers several health benefits as a natural sweetener, including acting as a digestive aid, boosting immunity, and providing essential minerals and antioxidants.
